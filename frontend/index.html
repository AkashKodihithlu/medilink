Features

Everything you need to allocate care smarter

From patient registration to emergency dispatch — MediLink handles the full healthcare resource lifecycle.

🧑‍⚕️

Patient Management

Register patients with urgency levels, location, and consultation preferences. Color-coded priority ensures critical cases are never missed.

🩺

Doctor Registry

Maintain a live registry of doctors and specialists with availability, location, and telemedicine capability. Filter and search in real time.

⚡

Smart Allocation Engine

AI-powered matching scores doctors on specialization fit, proximity, and availability. Finds the best match in seconds, not hours.

📡

Telemedicine Support

Automatically identifies tele-enabled doctors for remote consultations, skipping unnecessary transport for eligible patients.

🚑

Emergency Transport

Dispatch ambulances and mobile clinics with ETA tracking. Transport is automatically matched by proximity and case severity.

🗺️

Coverage Gap Analyzer

Identify underserved regions, missing specializations, and resource shortages through live analytics computed from real data.
