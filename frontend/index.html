How it Works

From registration to allocation in 3 steps

No complex setup. Add your data and let the system do the matching.

1

Register Patients & Doctors

Add patient details with location, condition, urgency, and consultation type. Maintain a live doctor registry with specializations and real-time availability.

2

System Finds the Best Match

The allocation engine scores available doctors against patient needs — specialization, location, urgency, and telemedicine preference all factor in.

3

Confirm & Dispatch

Review the match, confirm allocation. Doctor is marked busy, patient marked allocated, and the dashboard updates instantly across all pages.

About Us

Closing the rural healthcare gap with data

Access to quality healthcare in rural and remote areas is severely limited — uneven distribution of medical professionals, lack of specialists, insufficient emergency transport, and poor coordination between facilities.

MediPredict is a centralized, database-driven system that tracks patients, healthcare providers, facilities, equipment, telemedicine slots, and transport across rural regions — and intelligently allocates them in real time.

✓
Allocation of doctors and specialists based on real availability and patient needs
✓

✓

✓
Data-driven identification of underserved regions and specialist shortages
